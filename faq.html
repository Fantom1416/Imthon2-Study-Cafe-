FAQ

Answers to commonly asked questions from our users

How do I join the team?

Check out the home page and click apply to get to the applications page for the google forms! Wed love to have

you on our team. We have several positions available based on what you want to help out with!

Do I get service hours for joining?

Social media creators, moderators, tutors, blog team, and essay editors all receive service hours.

How do community service hours work for volunteers?

Every school records them differently! Our volunteer manager works with all the individual content creators to

make sure they get credit for their hard work in whatever way is required. We will sign off on as many hours as

you work up until a certain extent. We do reserve the right to retract any hours if content is plagiarized.

Are you a 501c3?

Yup! We have our 501c3 certification, and were an official Non-Profit organization.

How can I stay updated?

We post all the new information youll need to know on our Instagram and the website.
